Software Development

The software development team creates FintecNEWS digital products that are sold by subscription to customers. The team develops both websites (and ChatBot Development) as well as mobile apps. They also support the broadcast and podcast teams in producing livestream programming.

The developers also create eLearning videos and online help documentation to assist customers in the use of products. Online help is created with Adobe Captivate and Adobe RoboHelp applications.

These applications focus on the following industries:

  • Online Banking
  • Travel Planning and Reservations
  • Digital Wallets
  • Personal Accounting such as tax software
  • Investment Monitoring
