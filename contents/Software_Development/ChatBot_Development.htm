ChatBot Development

There are three elements that bring content to chatbot users.

  1. Creating the Content. That is done using microcontent.
  2. Chatbot engine for the Chatbot Client, such as Google's Dialogflow supported by  Adobe RoboHelp.
  3. Your chosen Chatbot Client such as Alexa, Siri or Facebook Messenger.  Adobe RoboHelp also provides a sample agent so that you can try things out.

The Chatbot Output has connectors to allow Google Dialogflow integration. For other chatbot engines such as IBM Watson and Alexa Lex, the content will be put into a format that developers can pick up to push to the chatbot engines.

Chatbots and virtual assistants

A computer program capable of holding textual or auditory conversations with humans and uses artificial intelligence to understand context and improve responses. 

Natural language processing (NLP) is quickly becoming the norm for customer interaction.
