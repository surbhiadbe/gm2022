Travel Planning and Reservations

Fintech and payments are viewed as a high priority for increased investment by the travel industry. It's an effective way to improve the traveller experience and increase sales.  Technology is changing how money moves and investment is rapidly rising. The goal of "frictionless travel" is accomplished by making buyer payments easier.

In a highly mobile, globally based customer base, standardizing financial transactions is complicated by the lack of a shared, global currency. Fintech can solve these problems.

 “Buy now, pay later” (BNPL) payment methods present a huge opportunity for the travel domain. Innovative financing arrangements let travelers choose from a wider range of hotel and transportation providers by delaying payment.
