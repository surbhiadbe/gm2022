Welcome on board FintecNEWS

FintecNEWS is a fictional company used in this sample project provided as a learning exercise for technical authors using Adobe RoboHelp 2022. The project is not for distribution or publication.

Joining our FintecNEWS team

You are joining an news-gathering organization that that focuses on burgeoning world of financial technology. Our employees use their communications creativity and talent to invent new solutions. We sincerely hope you will take pride in being an important part of FintecNEWS' success.

Please take time to review this knowledge base. If you have questions, feel free to ask your supervisor or to contact the Human Resources (HR) department. 

Your role here may be as a: 

  1. Writer or graphic artist involved in creating content for Fintech websites.
  2. Software developer to code our websites and smartphone apps with the latest charts and financial data.
  3. Podcast talent to produce engaging video livestreams.
  4. Administrative support for the communications team!

From our customer's perspective

From tapping your credit card on a Point of Sale terminal and the grocery store to signing your home mortgage closing papers online - welcome to the world of the cashless society!

The financial technologies (Fintech) that make the "mobile economy" possible are what our company is all about.

We provide up to date information to software and hardware developers around the world.

They get the information by subscribing to our various digitally published products. Among the industries we report on are:

  • Online Banking
  • Travel Planning and Reservations
  • Digital Wallets
  • Personal Accounting such as tax software
  • Investment Monitoring
