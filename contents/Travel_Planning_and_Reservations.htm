Travel Planning and Reservations

Here are country-specific guidelines for these office locations.

For the United Kingdom: Fintech and payments are viewed as a high priority for increased investment by the travel industry. It's an effective way to improve the traveler experience and increase sales.  Technology is changing how money moves and investment is rapidly rising. The goal of "frictionless travel" is accomplished by making buyer payments easier.

For the United States: In a highly mobile, globally based customer base, standardizing financial transactions is complicated by the lack of a shared, global currency. Fintech can solve these problems.

 For Canada: “Buy now, pay later” (BNPL) payment methods present a huge opportunity for the travel domain. Innovative financing arrangements let travelers choose from a wider range of hotel and transportation providers by delaying payment.  NOTE: "Buy now, pay later" payment methods may not be available in all countries.
